Firmware Update:

Download the latest firmware here: https://www.endorphin.es/modules/p/two-of-cups

  • Power OFF you modular system.
  • Eject the microSD card from the module. Insert it into the computer.
  • Connect the audio output from your computer headphones output or phone to one of the audio inputs on the module with simple mono or stereo cable.
  • Unpack the downloaded archive and place the toc-firmware.hex file into the root folder of the microSD card. Don't rename the file in any other name. Put the card back into the module and turn ON the power of your modular system.
  • The LED will light up in fuchsia colour and you will hear an ascending tone from VOICE 1 OUT. After approximately 10 seconds the module will reboot with the new firmware installed.
