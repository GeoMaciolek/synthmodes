Modes:

Summary

To switch the functionality of the Parameter knobs press the Bank button to scan through 6 available modes shown by the LED.

  • BLUE - Sample decay
  • DIM BLUE - Sample volume
  • RED - Sample pitch
  • DIM RED - Sample Looping, disable by setting the Param knob CCW and to enable - fully CW.
  • FUCHSIA - Sample Selection over available samples, up to 24 samples.
  • DIM FUCHSIA - Sample reverse, disable by setting Param knob CCW and to enable - fully CW.

To save the position of the Parameter knob in each mode, quickly scan through all the modes by short pressing the Bank button. The next time you power up the module the settings will be remembered independently for each bank.
