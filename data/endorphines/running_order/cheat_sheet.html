Cheat Sheet for things you might forget:

  • To switch between SH101 and Euclidean mode enter the track edit view by flipping the switch either to the Left (Track 1) or to the Right (Track 2) and then hold press either the Step button (for Track 1) or Rest button (for Track 2). Euclidean mode will be shown by Fuchsia colour of the Illuminati LED.


  • The primary pattern modifier for Sh101 mode is ratcheting per step, the amount of ratchets is controlled by Repeats 1 for Track 1 and by Repeats 2 for Track 2.


  • The secondary pattern modifier for SH101 mode is probability per step and is controlled by Repeats 2 for Track 1 ad by Repeats 1 for track 2.


  • The primary pattern modifier for Euclidean mode is pattern rotation, which is controlled by Repeats 1 for Track 1 and by Repeats 2 for Track 2.


  • The secondary pattern modifier for Euclidean mode is global trigger probability, which is controlled by Repeats 2 for Track 1 and Repeats 1 for Track 2.


  • To switch the pattern per track hold press Step + Rest in Live mode and turn knobs Repeats 1 and Repeats 2 to switch the patterns for Track 1 and Track 2 respectively.
