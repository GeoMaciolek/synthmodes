Running Order

Running Order is a two track trigger sequencer / clock utility in 6 hp. It can be a precise master clock with Zero-jitter clock generation or manipulate incoming clock signal by acting as a divider / multiplier. Punch in your sequences by either using the SH-101 style input or Euclidean mode, with a variety of ways to modulate your patterns both internally and externally. Ability to create tuplets and odd time signatures makes this the ultimate clock / rhythm mangling utility for your eurorack system.
