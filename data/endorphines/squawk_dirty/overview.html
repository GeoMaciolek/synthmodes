Overview

Squawk Dirty To Me is a simple and effective 6hp stereo VCF with 8 different filter types, that can also be sequenced with external CV. High quality 96kHz 16 bit stereo processing along with zero-delay feedback DSP makes it the perfect tool for modern musicians and sound designers. Built in post filter VCA allows one to have a compact voice setup. Additional hi-pass filter cuts boomy low end and all pass filter delay on the right output letâ€™s you widen your stereo image.

The following filter types are available: 24DB/OCT TRANSISTOR LADDER, MS-20 12DB/OCT, 12DB/OCT LOW PASS GATE, 12DB/OCT RESONANT LOW PASS GATE, 12DB/OCT DJ ISOLATOR BIPOLAR VARIABLE FILTER, 12DB/OCT HIGH PASS STATE VARIABLE FILTER, 12DB/OCT BAND PASS STATE VARIABLE FILTER, COMB FILTER / RESONATOR.
