Operation Modes:

Summary

Total Recall has 2 main operation modes: Preset Manager and Macro. Each mode has 2 options for external control with either CV or triggers / clocks. To switch the sub modes for CV / Trigger functionality hold-press the Sample button for 1 second. To switch between Preset Manager mode and Macro mode hold-press the Sample button for 3+ seconds.

Preset manager with meta CV scan over the presets:

One LED lights up at a time, showing the currently selected preset (LED is fully on for presets I-IV, semi lit for presets V-VIII). Applying CV (0...5V) into META CV will scan through the presets. Depending on the SMOOTH switch the morphing will either be instant or continuous.

Preset manager with meta TRIGGER (strobe):

LEDs indication is similar as in mode 1, but inverted. Applying trigger into META will switch to the next preset. Depending on the SMOOTH switch the change will either be instant or slewed by 100ms.

Macro CV generator with meta CV min - max morphing:

OLED 4 is constantly fully lit indicating macro CV mode. With short MODE press we select CV channel 1-3 to define its min/max values with the knobs 2 and 1 respectively. Applying CV into META CV will morph between min. and max states.

Macro CV generator with meta TRIGGER (strobe):

LEDs indication is similar as in mode 3, but LED 4 is now semi-lit. Applying a single trigger to META CV will jump from min. to max. like the flip-flop function. Depending on the SMOOTH switch the change will either be instant or slewed by 100ms.
