Total Recall

Total Recall is a 3 channel preset manager / multistage and macro controller for eurorack synthesisers in 6hp. It allows you to send 3 precise voltages per each of the 8 preset slots, scroll through them manually or via external CV / trigger sources. Macro mode lets you send 3 CVs with span controlled with a master macro knob. Additionally you can process external CVs and audio sources through internally controlled VCAs / Polarisers for new modulation shapes / tones or general utility purposes.
