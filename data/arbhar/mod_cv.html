Reverb

MOD CV controls a reverb.

Positive voltage mixes reverb with dry signal.
Reverb freezes at +5V

Negative voltage omits the dry signal and only allows the wet signal to pass. The reverb has additional shimmering modulation.

Delay

MOD CV controls a delay.

Positive voltage controls delay times between 80-1000ms with fixed feedback level of 67% for a more standard echo effect.

Negative voltage controls delay times between 25-1ms with a fixed feedback level of 99% for some shimmering KarplusStrong effects.

A summed dry and wet signal will generate from Output 1 if it's the only Output connected.

When both are connected:
Output 1: Dry signal.
Output 2: Wet signal.
