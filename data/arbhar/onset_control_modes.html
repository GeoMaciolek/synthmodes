Description

Hold Shift and press Capture to change the onset capture mode then turning the layer knob.

Modes

Alpha

Capture Behaviour:
Capture activates upon onset detection.

Gate Output:
Beginning of each grain.
Beta

Capture Behaviour:
Capture activates upon onset detection.

Gate Output:
Beginning of each onset detection and is high for duration of time set by the Hold parameter.
Gamma

Capture Behaviour:
Capture will not activate upon onset detection.

Gate Output:
Beginning of each grain.
Delta

Capture Behaviour:
Capture will not activate upon onset detection.

Gate Output:
Beginning of each onset detection and is high for duration of time set by the Hold parameter.
