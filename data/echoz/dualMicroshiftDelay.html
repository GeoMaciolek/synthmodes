Mode

Description

A mono delay line into a pair of micro pitch shifters. The pitch shifting is fixed to a slight detune on one side and small shift up on the other. The Filter control blends between the two with all the way counter-clockwise being only downward to only upward shift on the full clockwise. Both pitch shifters are in the feedback path so the pitch will continue to rise or drop as the repeats decay.

Controls

TIME Delay time
FILTER Mix of downward (CCW) to upward (CW) tuning
FEEDBACK Amount of feedback into the delay line
