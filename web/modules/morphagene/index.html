Description

Hold Splice, then press Rec to enter/exit Reel Mode.
While in Reel Mode, use Organize to pick Reel.
To delete current Reel, hold Shift and press Rec. Deleting all Reels, or removing microSD, also exits Reel Mode.

Firmware v2

Hold Rec, and press Shift to step through the gain settings:

  • -3db (blue)
  • 0db (green)
  • +6db (orange)
  • +12db (purple)

Firmware v1

Hold Rec, then press Shift.

Internal gain will be set to normalize incoming audio.

Audio is continually monitored while Rec is held.
